Diaries of a Globetrotter

“To Travel is to Live” – Hans Christian Andersen

An adventurer and an amateur photographer who loves to experience and capture memories with a lens.

Greenland

Travelling to Greenland - the most nordic location I have ever been is a dream come true. Dog sledding, snow-mobiling and tasting rare delicacies such as the whale, reindeer and muskbox are some of those experiences that I will never get anywhere else.

Komodo Island

Komodo is one of the 17,508 islands that compose the Republic of Indonesia. The island is particularly notable as the habitat of the Komodo dragon, the largest lizard on Earth, which is named after the island.

Berlin

Berlin is the capital and the largest city of Germany as well as one of its 16 constituent states. With a population of approximately 3.7 million, Berlin is the second most populous city proper in the European Union and the seventh most populous urban area in the European Union.
